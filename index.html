About Dr. Lannin

Dr. Janani Lannin established her optometric practice in the Northstate over fifteen years ago. As a mother of three and a long-time Redding resident, Dr. Lannin has created a practice that accomplishes a seamless fusion of state-of-the-art technologies and old-fashioned, quality service. During her time here, her practice has grown many-fold, solely by positive referrals and word-of-mouth. She is passionate about facilitating optimal vision for her patients, while also ensuring their ocular health.

Professional Education
